Frequently asked questions

What happens after I submit my email address to subscribe to eBulletins?

If you are a new eBulletin subscriber you:

  • Will be prompted to confirm your email address and enter an optional password
  • have the option to subscribe to other eBulletins
  • can also choose to answer some additional questions about yourself and your organisation. These will help us tailor our eBulletins to your requirements
  • will also be given the option to subscribe to emails from other public bodies including BSI, DECC and BIS.

If you are an existing subscriber to HSE e-Bulletins you will have the opportunity to update and amend your existing subscriptions.

How will eBulletin newsletters and notifications help me?

They will keep you informed about:

  • Latest news and guidance from a range of almost 100 topics and industries
  • Recent additions/amendments or improvements to the HSE website
  • New and updated HSE publications
  • Recently issued HSE press releases
  • News about HSE events and initiatives

How much do HSE eBulletins cost?

HSE eBulletins are a free service.

Can I change which eBulletins I receive?

Yes, just follow the "Manage Preferences" link at the foot of each eBulletin.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, just follow the "Manage Preferences" link at the foot of each eBulletin.

Can I unsubscribe?

Yes, just follow the link at the foot of each eBulletin.
