Managing my asbestos

A step by step guide to the duty to manage asbestos

This systematic guide takes duty holders through the process of understanding their duties as defined in Regulation 4 of the Control of Asbestos Regulations 2012.

A set of questions will enable you to identify whether you are a duty holder and take you through a series of steps to help you decide if there is asbestos present and if so, how to manage it.

It is visual and easy to follow, but it takes several visits to complete.  This is accessible guidance, intended to be user friendly and will save you time in understanding your duties.
