Poultry dust in farming

Poultry farming is a dusty business. In the enclosed environment of the poultry shed, poultry farm workers may be exposed to high levels of airborne organic dust (poultry dust).

Non-technical information on how to prevent respiratory disease from working with poultry and tell you where you can get further help, if you need it.
