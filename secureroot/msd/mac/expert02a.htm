Expert scores for carrying from conveyor to store task

Risk Factors Score Reason

Load weight

A = 4

25 kg box one carry every 20 seconds (approx.)
Possible solutions

Hand distance from lower back

G = 0

No problems

Asymmetrical torso/ load

A = 1

Example of holding a load with one hand therefore amber
Possible solutions

Postural constraints

A = 1

Restricted posture as the operative must negotiate and alter his posture to get under the pipe. Not severe as working in an attic etc.
Possible solutions

Grip on load

A = 1

This is A/1 as he can hold and support it firmly to the body
Possible solutions

Floor surface

G = 0

No problems

Carry distance

A = 1

Definitely between 4m -10m
Possible solutions

Obstacles on route

A = 2

Some trip hazards laid around the floor in the corridor
Possible solutions

Environmental factors

None

No problems
